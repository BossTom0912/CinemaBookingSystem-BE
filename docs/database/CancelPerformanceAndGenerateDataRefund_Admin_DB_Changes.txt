============================================================
1. Nhanh CancelPerformanceAndGenerateDataRefund
============================================================

Nhanh nay bo sung luong huy suat chieu, tao refund, khach hang bo sung thong tin
hoan tien va admin xu ly hoan tien thu cong.

1.1. Bang moi duoc them

- BANK_DIRECTORY
  Luu danh muc ngan hang ho tro hoan tien.
  Cac cot chinh:
  bankCode, bankBin, shortName, fullName, isActive,
  supportsAccountInquiry, supportsPayout, createdAt, updatedAt.

- REFUND_CLAIM
  Luu thong tin khach hang khai bao de nhan tien hoan.
  Lien ket 1-1 voi REFUND va lien ket voi CUSTOMER_PROFILE.
  Co the lien ket BANK_DIRECTORY qua bankCode.
  Cac thong tin nhay cam nhu so tai khoan/ten chu tai khoan duoc thiet ke de luu encrypted.

- REFUND_CLAIM_TOKEN
  Luu token hash cho link khach hang khai bao thong tin hoan tien.
  Chi luu tokenHash, khong luu raw token.

- CUSTOMER_REFUND_REQUEST
  Luu request hoan tien/ho tro tu khach hang, gan voi REFUND, CUSTOMER_PROFILE
  va co the gan voi TICKET.

- MANUAL_REFUND_PROCESS
  Luu quy trinh admin xu ly hoan tien thu cong.
  Co thong tin nguoi duoc assign, trang thai xu ly, ma giao dich ngan hang,
  so tien da chuyen, proofUrl, ghi chu admin, thoi diem assign/confirm.

- REFUND_PAYOUT_ATTEMPT
  Luu cac lan thu payout qua provider/ngan hang.
  Ho tro idempotencyKey, attemptNumber, provider request/transaction code,
  trang thai attempt va failureReason.

- EMAIL_OUTBOX
  Luu email can gui/retry, payload duoc thiet ke de ma hoa truoc khi persist.
  Ho tro retry qua outboxStatus, attemptCount, nextAttemptAt, lastError.

1.2. Constraint va index moi quan trong

- UQ_BANK_DIRECTORY_BIN
- UQ_REFUND_CLAIM_REFUND
- UQ_REFUND_CLAIM_TOKEN_HASH
- UQ_MANUAL_REFUND_PROCESS_REFUND
- UQ_MANUAL_REFUND_PROCESS_CLAIM
- UX_MANUAL_REFUND_BANK_TRANSACTION_CODE
- IX_REFUND_CLAIM_CUSTOMER_PROFILE_ID
- IX_REFUND_CLAIM_STATUS
- IX_REFUND_CLAIM_TOKEN_CLAIM
- IX_CUSTOMER_REFUND_REQUEST_CUSTOMER_STATUS
- IX_REFUND_PAYOUT_ATTEMPT_REFUND_STATUS
- IX_MANUAL_REFUND_PROCESS_STATUS_CREATED
- IX_EMAIL_OUTBOX_STATUS_NEXT_ATTEMPT

1.3. Status moi/bo sung

- REFUND.refundStatus:
  co them/ho tro REQUESTED ben canh cac trang thai PENDING, PROCESSING,
  SUCCESS, FAILED.

- REFUND_CLAIM.claimStatus:
  PENDING_INFO, VERIFIED, SUBMITTED, PROCESSING, COMPLETED,
  EXPIRED, MANUAL_REQUIRED, REVOKED.

- REFUND_CLAIM.accountValidationStatus:
  NOT_STARTED, VERIFIED, FAILED, UNAVAILABLE.

- MANUAL_REFUND_PROCESS.processStatus:
  OPEN, IN_PROGRESS, CONFIRMED, REJECTED.

- REFUND_PAYOUT_ATTEMPT.attemptStatus:
  CREATED, SUBMITTED, ACCEPTED, CONFIRMED, FAILED, UNKNOWN.

- EMAIL_OUTBOX.outboxStatus:
  PENDING, PROCESSING, SENT, FAILED.

============================================================
2. Nhanh Admin
============================================================

Nhanh nay bo sung DB cho cac chuc nang admin/movie CRUD, thong ke view,
highlight phim, moderation review, lich su review va chatbot.

2.1. Cot moi trong MOVIE

- highlight
  Phan loai phim noi bat.
  Gia tri hop le: HOT, NEW, TRENDING.

- viewCount
  Dem tong luot xem phim.

- averageRating
  Diem rating trung binh.

- totalReviews
  Tong so review.

- totalViews
  Tong so luot xem dung cho thong ke/highlight.

- dailyViews
  Luot xem trong ngay.

2.2. Cot moi trong USER

- spamViolationCount
  Dem so lan vi pham spam review.

- isBlocked
  Danh dau user bi block.

- blockedUntil
  Thoi diem block het han.

2.3. Bang moi duoc them

- CHAT_HISTORY
  Luu lich su tin nhan chatbot theo user/session.

- MOVIE_VIEW_LOG
  Luu log moi lan xem phim, gom movie, user neu co, thoi gian xem va IP.

- MOVIE_DAILY_VIEW
  Luu thong ke luot xem theo phim va theo ngay.

- REVIEW_EDIT_HISTORY
  Luu lich su sua review: old/new rating, old/new comment, editedAt.

- REVIEW_MODERATION_HISTORY
  Luu lich su moderation review: oldStatus, newStatus, moderatorId,
  rejectedReason, moderatedAt.

2.4. Thay doi review

- REVIEW duoc lien ket voi BOOKING qua bookingId.
- Them unique index UX_REVIEW_BOOKING de tranh 1 booking tao nhieu review.
- Them cac bang lich su edit/moderation de audit qua trinh sua/duyet/reject review.

2.5. Constraint va index moi quan trong

- CK_MOVIE_HIGHLIGHT
  Gioi han highlight chi gom HOT, NEW, TRENDING.

- IX_REVIEW_EDIT_HISTORY_REVIEW_ID
- IX_REVIEW_MODERATION_HISTORY_REVIEW_ID
- IX_MOVIE_DAILY_VIEW_DATE
- IX_MOVIE_HIGHLIGHT_VIEWS
- UX_REVIEW_BOOKING

============================================================
3. DB sau khi gop 2 nhanh se ho tro gi?
============================================================

- Huy movie/showtime/room co booking paid va chuyen booking sang REFUND_PENDING.
- Tao REFUND va theo doi trang thai refund.
- Khach hang nhan link claim refund va nhap thong tin ngan hang.
- Admin xem, assign va confirm manual refund.
- Theo doi cac lan payout/refund attempt voi idempotency.
- Luu outbox email de retry gui email lien quan den refund.
- Quan ly danh muc ngan hang phuc vu hoan tien.
- Quan ly movie admin CRUD voi highlight, view count, rating aggregate.
- Log luot xem phim va thong ke daily view.
- Tao/quan ly review gan voi booking.
- Luu lich su sua review va moderation review.
- Block user spam review.
- Luu lich su chatbot.
